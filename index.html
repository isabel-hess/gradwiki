Fish Robots, Soft Actuators
& Electrostatic Energy Harvesting

A multi-year experimental and computational program designing, building, and characterizing a HASEL-actuated soft robotic fish — and demonstrating electricity generation from artificial muscles for the first time.

🎯 Dissertation Aims
Aim 1
Soft Robotic Fish — Nebula
Design, fabrication, and experimental characterization of a continuously flexible, HASEL-actuated robotic trout. Kinematic analysis in quiescent water with DIC + LDV. EHE beam model for quiescent and uniform flow.
Journal Published RoboSoft 2025
Explore Nebula →
Aim 2
HASEL Energy Harvesting
First experimental demonstration of electricity generation from Peano-HASEL actuators. Constant-voltage cycle, circuit design, 2.49 mJ/pouch, 23% mechanical efficiency. Frequency dependence, self-sensing, memcapacitance.
Paper 1 Published Paper 2 In Progress
